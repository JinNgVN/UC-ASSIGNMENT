OUR TEAS

RASPBERRY & LEMON

Our Raspberry & Lemon creates a unique and
complex flavour profile that is both sweet and tangy,
with a subtle hint of tartness that perfectly balances
out the sweetness. It is a perfect choice for anyone
looking for a refreshing and rejuvenating beverage.

INGREDIENTS:
Hibiscus, Apple, Orange peels, Raspberry and lemon flavouring (7%),
Raspberry pieces (0.1%).
