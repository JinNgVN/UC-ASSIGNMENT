OUR TEAS

RASPBERRY & LEMON

Our Raspberry & Lemon creates a unique and complex flavour profile that is both sweet and tangy, with a subtle hint of tartness that perfectly balances out the sweetness. It is a perfect choice for anyone looking for a refreshing and rejuvenating beverage.

INGREDIENTS:
Hibiscus, Apple, Orange peels, Raspberry and lemon flavouring (7%), Raspberry pieces (0.1%).

ORANGE & CINNAMON

Discover the citrusy flavour of orange, with hints of cinnamon and the beautiful amber brew from 100% sustainably sourced Rooibos from South Africa in this warming blend.

INGREDIENTS:
Orange peel, Rooibos, Vietnamese cinnamon, Orange flavouring.

PURE CAMOMILE

Sink into a deep armchair and enjoy a moment of calm with our gently soothing Pure Camomile Infusion. 100% sustainably sourced camomile is harvested during the warm summer months to create a golden infusion which is naturally slightly sweet and floral.

INGREDIENTS:
Camomile.

GREEN TEA & MINT

Add a fresh minty twist to your day. We think that our Green Tea & Mint are perfectly matched. The mint softens the taste of green tea so it’s deliciously light and refreshing. Perfect any time you want to bring a little moment of Zen into your day.

INGREDIENTS:
Green tea, Mint leaves, Natural flavouring.

RUSSIAN CARRAVAN

The distinctively smooth smokiness of summer harvested Chinese Keemun teas in this blend is reminiscent of tea camel caravans travelling to Russia in the 19th century. During the journey, the tea absorbed the aroma of the campfires giving it a subtle smokiness, hence inspiring this blend’s name.

INGREDIENTS:
Black tea.

PURE EARL GREY

Light and aromatic, with a citrusy twist, for those who travel off the beaten track. First created by Twinings in 1831 for the British Prime Minister the 2nd Earl Grey of England, this iconic loose leaf blend perfectly balances citrusy bergamot flavour with a gentle black tea base.

INGREDIENTS:
Black tea.
